Overview

This Expanded Beam Optics Multi-Source Agreement Group aims to standardize EBO connectors for wide scale adoption, enabling multiple connector vendors to supply industry.

The group is free to join, prospective members should use the contact form to register interest and send a signed copy of the MSA Agreement (link below).
